# Assignment 05 — Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## Task
Write a CoT prompt with one worked example, then use it to solve this problem:

> A freelancer charges ₹1,500 per hour. She works 6 hours on Project A and 4 hours
> on Project B. She gives a 10% discount to Project B's client.
> What is her total earnings for the day?

The worked example you provide must be a **different** maths or business problem — not the same one above.

## Rules
- The worked example must show numbered reasoning steps
- The final answer must be clearly labelled "Answer:"
- The model must follow the same numbered format for the new problem

## What will be checked
- A different worked example is included as the CoT demonstration
- The model shows numbered steps (not just a final number)
- Final answer is correct: ₹14,400
- Script runs cleanly
